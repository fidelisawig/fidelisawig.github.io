Project Overview

Developed interactive dashboards to visualize national sanitation indicators, directly supporting the Ministry's National Roadmap on Domestic Wastewater Management and enabling evidence-based policy decision-making.

Key Features

Structured Data Management: Designed and maintained a cross-municipal database to track sanitation infrastructure while improving data integrity, security, and cross-divisional access.

Analytical Reporting: Produced high-level narratives and technical summaries that translated complex data trends into actionable insight for government stakeholders.

Impact

Data-Driven Policy Formulation

Translated raw national sanitation indicators into high-impact visual narratives for ministerial leadership.

Operational Efficiency

Streamlined infrastructure tracking through a centralized database and reduced cross-divisional data silos.

Stakeholder Empowerment

Improved stakeholder capacity to interpret spatial and sectoral data for targeted resource allocation.

Aceh Districts Risk Map

Spatial risk modeling and disaster contingency mapping for district-level planning within a UN intergovernmental framework.

ArcMap Remote Sensing QGIS Risk Modeling
Role
GIS Assistant
Technologies
  • ArcMap
  • Data Driven Page
  • Remote Sensing (ENVI)
  • QGIS
Scale
7 districts mapped; 6 hazard categories; 7 contingency plan documents

Project Overview

Spearheaded spatial risk modeling and provided critical geospatial analysis for District Disaster Management and Contingency Plans, delivering evidence-based insights for disaster policy decision-making.

Key Features

Methodological Compliance: Applied the Perka 3/2012 BNPB regulatory framework to keep risk assessment standardized across districts.

Advanced Quantitative Analysis: Conducted cross-analysis using MS Excel PivotTables to extract actionable patterns from complex disaster risk datasets.

Impact

Enhanced Disaster Preparedness

Converted complex geospatial hazard data into risk maps that contributed to 7 district-level contingency plans.

Regulatory Alignment

Supported consistent BNPB Perka 3/2012 implementation across regional planning workflows.

Strategic Policy Support

Equipped local government decision-makers with visual tools for resource allocation and emergency response planning.

Interactive Map Designer

A web-based cartographic tool for designing and exporting publication-ready maps with customizable layouts, projections, and styling.

Project Overview

Wilayah Studi is a React and TypeScript application that helps cartographers and GIS professionals design publication-ready maps. It combines a responsive canvas, projection controls, and export-oriented layout tools.

Key Features

Projection Support: Toggle between map projections to evaluate geographic distortion and presentation choices.

Dynamic Styling: Customize colors, typography, and spatial layers with real-time preview.

Export Options: Generate publication-ready SVG outputs for reports, articles, and technical documentation.
